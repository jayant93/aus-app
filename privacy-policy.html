Privacy Policy

AUS — Are You Satisfied?

Last Updated: November 10, 2025
Privacy First: AUS is designed with your privacy as a core principle. All your reflection data, satisfaction ratings, and notes are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

AUS stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Satisfaction ratings: Your 1-5 ratings for each reflection prompt
  • Reflection notes: Any text notes you choose to add
  • Timestamps: When you responded to prompts
  • App settings: Your chosen reminder interval, active hours, and notification preferences

This data remains on your device and is never automatically transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

1.2 Data We May Collect (Optional)

If you choose to contact us or sign up for updates:

  • Email address: Only if you voluntarily provide it for updates, support, or early access
  • Device information: Basic device model and OS version for troubleshooting support requests

1.3 Analytics and Crash Reporting

AUS uses minimal analytics to improve the app:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., app opens, feature usage)
  • Crash reports to fix bugs (containing no personal data)
  • No tracking of your reflection content, ratings, or notes

These are aggregated and anonymized. You can opt out in the app settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Data

Your satisfaction ratings and reflection notes are used exclusively on your device to:

  • Display trends and patterns in your satisfaction over time
  • Help you identify what activities bring meaning and satisfaction
  • Generate personal insights about your work patterns

2.2 Contact Information

If provided, your email address is used only to:

  • Send requested app updates or early access information
  • Respond to support inquiries
  • Communicate important app changes (very infrequently)

We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

AUS takes data security seriously:

  • Local storage: All reflection data is stored in your device's secure app storage, protected by Android's app sandbox
  • No cloud sync: By default, your data never leaves your device
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using Android's encryption APIs
  • Backups: If you enable device backups, your data may be included in encrypted Android backups

4. Data Export and Deletion

4.1 Your Data, Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Export all your data in JSON or CSV format at any time from the app settings
  • Delete: Delete individual entries or all data permanently from within the app
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling AUS removes all locally stored data from your device

4.2 Optional Cloud Backup

If you enable optional cloud backup features in future versions:

  • You will be clearly informed and must explicitly opt-in
  • Data will be encrypted end-to-end
  • You can delete cloud data at any time

5. Third-Party Services

AUS may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates (governed by Google's privacy policy)
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics (can be disabled in settings)
  • Crashlytics: For crash reporting without personal data

These services are configured to minimize data collection and do not access your reflection content.

6. Children's Privacy

AUS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email if you've provided one.

8. International Users

AUS is designed for global use. Since data is stored locally on your device, international data transfer concerns are minimal. Any data you voluntarily share with us may be processed in India or other locations where we operate.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to your data (via the app's export feature)
  • Correction of inaccurate data (via the app's edit features)
  • Deletion of your data (via the app's delete features)
  • Objection to data processing (by disabling analytics)
  • Data portability (via export features)

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@example.com
  • General inquiries: hello@example.com

We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

Philosophy: AUS is built on the principle that meaningful reflection requires privacy. Your thoughts, ratings, and insights are yours alone. We're here to provide the tool, not to monetize your data.
